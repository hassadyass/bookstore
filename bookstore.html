Classics

Anna Karenina

"Anna Karenina" is a classic novel by Leo Tolstoy that follows the tragic love affair of Anna Karenina, a married aristocrat, and Count Vronsky. Set in 19th-century Russia, the story explores themes of love, society, and morality with rich characterization and profound depth.

Lord Of The Rings

"The Lord of the Rings" is a renowned high fantasy novel by J.R.R. Tolkien, chronicling Frodo Baggins' quest to destroy a dangerous ring that threatens Middle-earth. It's celebrated for its rich world-building, epic battles, and themes of heroism and friendship.

Pride And Prejudice

"Pride and Prejudice" by Jane Austen is a classic romance novel that follows Elizabeth Bennet's journey of self-discovery and love in 19th-century England.

Sense And Sensibility

"Sense and Sensibility" by Jane Austen tells the story of the Dashwood sisters, Elinor and Marianne, as they navigate love, heartbreak, and societal expectations in early 19th-century England.

Romance

Things We Hide From The Light

"Things We Hide from the Light" by Lucy Score is a contemporary romance novel that delves into themes of personal secrets, emotional vulnerability, and the transformative power of love.

Things We Left Behind

"Things We Left Behind" by Lucy Score is a contemporary romance about Dax Pierce, a former NFL player turned coach, who finds love with Harlow James, a bookstore owner and single mother, as they navigate personal challenges and discover second chances in a small town setting.

Twisted Lies

"Twisted Lies" by Ana Huang is a gripping romantic suspense novel about Olivia Jones, whose relationship with the wealthy Alexander Burnham uncovers dangerous secrets and deception, testing their love amidst intrigue and suspense.

Twisted Love

"Twisted Love" by Ana Huang is a gripping contemporary romance that explores intricate relationships and the transformative power of love amidst unexpected challenges.

Horror

Dracula

"Dracula" by Bram Stoker is a Gothic horror novel that follows the story of Count Dracula's attempt to move from Transylvania to England so he may find new blood and spread the undead curse, and the battle between Dracula and a small group of people led by Professor Abraham Van Helsing

Hell House

"Hell House" by Richard Matheson is a horror novel about a group of investigators who spend a week in the infamous Belasco House, also known as "Hell House," to study its haunted reputation.

The Exorcist

"The Exorcist" by William Peter Blatty is a horror novel that tells the story of a young girl named Regan MacNeil who becomes possessed by a malevolent entity. The novel follows the desperate attempts of her mother and two priests to save her through an exorcism.

The Haunting Of Hill House

"The Haunting of Hill House" by Shirley Jackson is a chilling novel about a group of people who investigate paranormal activity at a haunted mansion, uncovering its sinister secrets.

Poetry

Because I Could Not Stop For Death

"Because I Could Not Stop for Death" is a poem by Emily Dickinson that explores the theme of death personified as a courteous and patient escort, taking the speaker on a journey from life to eternity.

Teachings Of Rumi

Rumi's teachings emphasize love, unity, and spiritual growth, encouraging seekers to find the divine within and connect with all creation through love and devotion.

Twenty Love Poems And a Song of Despair

"Twenty Love Poems and a Song of Despair" is a collection of poetry by the Chilean poet Pablo Neruda. It explores themes of love, passion, desire, and heartbreak through vivid imagery and lyrical language, capturing the intensity and complexities of romantic relationships.

Unfortunately It Was Paradise

"Unfortunately, It Was Paradise" is a collection of poetry by Mahmoud Darwish, a Palestinian poet. The collection reflects on themes of identity, exile, love, loss, and resistance, offering poignant reflections on the Palestinian experience and broader human struggles for justice and freedom.

Philosophy

Thus Spoke Zarathustra

"Thus Spoke Zarathustra" is a philosophical novel by Friedrich Nietzsche that explores themes of the Übermensch (Overman), the eternal recurrence, and the nature of existence through the teachings of the character Zarathustra.

Being And Time

"Being and Time" is a seminal work of philosophy by Martin Heidegger. It delves into the nature of being (ontology) and the concept of Dasein (human existence) through an exploration of time, authenticity, and the existential conditions that shape our understanding of existence.

Critique of Pure Reason

"Critique of Pure Reason" is a foundational work of modern philosophy by Immanuel Kant. It examines the nature of human knowledge, exploring how our minds structure and interpret experience through concepts such as space, time, and causality.

Discourse on the Method And Meditations on First Philosophy

Descartes presents a method for acquiring knowledge based on doubt and rational thinking, introducing "I think, therefore I am" (Cogito, ergo sum) and explores foundational philosophical questions about existence, reality, and the nature of the mind, seeking to establish certain knowledge.

Other

The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

"The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy" is a comedic science fiction novel by Douglas Adams about Arthur Dent's adventures through space after Earth's destruction, guided by the eccentricities of the titular guidebook and its quirky characters.

Educated

"Educated" is a memoir by Tara Westover that recounts her journey from growing up in a strict, isolated family in rural Idaho to pursuing education and breaking free from her tumultuous past. It explores themes of family dynamics, resilience, and the transformative power of learning and self-discovery.

The Girl on the Train

"The Girl on the Train" is a psychological thriller novel by Paula Hawkins that revolves around Rachel Watson, who becomes involved in a mysterious missing persons case after witnessing something unsettling during her daily train ride.

The Night Circus

"The Night Circus" is a fantasy novel by Erin Morgenstern about a magical competition between two illusionists set within a mysterious circus that only appears at night.
